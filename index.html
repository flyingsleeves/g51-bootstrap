Overview

G-51 Capital Management is a premier, early stage venture capital firm that specializes in creating high value for our portfolio companies and delivering top returns to our investors. We lead Series A investments with $250k to $1 million and we syndicate Series A up to $3 million per round. We often syndicate our Series B investments with large institutional venture capital firms to provide the capital companies need to meet revised milestones or to extend their operational runway.

G-51 provides the sound experience, deep knowledge, and funding support that entrepreneurs and large venture funds need to close the operational, technical and financial gaps that open during the early process of building long-term commercially viable businesses. Based in Austin, Texas, G-51 invests nationally in the software, hardware, internet and cleantech sectors
